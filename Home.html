Over the years, we have analysed different Interview questions and hence we present you these carefully curated practice sets, below are few of the most important.
Subject-Specific Practice Sets

These sets are designed to help faculty and staff develop their subject-specific knowledge and skills.

Soft Skills Practice Sets

Soft skills are essential for effective communication, leadership, and teamwork. PDCs provide practice sets to help faculty and staff develop skills such as communication, time management, problem-solving, and conflict resolution.

Research Methodology Practice Sets

These sets can help faculty and staff develop their research skills. They include practice sets on research design, data analysis, literature review, and research ethics.

Administrative Procedure Practice Sets

These sets are designed to help faculty and staff develop their administrative skills. They include practice sets on grant writing, budget management, project management, and strategic planning.
